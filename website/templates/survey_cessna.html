Cessna 172 Specific Tips
  • RPM: Cruise typically 2300-2500 RPM, Full power 2700 RPM
  • Oil Temperature: Should stabilize around 180-200°F in cruise
  • Oil Pressure: Should be 20-60 PSI, typically 30-40 PSI in cruise
  • Cylinder Head Temp: Should be below 500°F, optimal 350-400°F
  • Fuel Pressure: Should be steady, fluctuations indicate issues
