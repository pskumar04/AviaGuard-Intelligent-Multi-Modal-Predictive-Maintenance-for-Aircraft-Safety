Critical Safety Considerations
  • Rotor RPM outside 300-400 RPM: Immediate grounding required
  • Vibration above 0.5 ips: Do not fly, rotor balance needed
  • Track & Balance above 0.1: Requires immediate maintenance
  • T4 temperature above 850°C: Engine damage risk, reduce power
  • Torque fluctuations: Indicates transmission or engine issues

Airbus H125 Specific Maintenance Tips
Rotor System
  • Check blade tracking every 50 flight hours
  • Balance rotor system if vibration exceeds 0.3 ips
  • Inspect rotor head for cracks regularly
  • Monitor lead-lag damper condition
Engine System
  • Monitor T4 trend - rising indicates wear
  • Check torque split between engines
  • Monitor oil consumption rate
  • Check compressor wash schedule
